Report a Concern

All contacts made through phonepeethics.com are received by the Global Ethics Office in Bentonville, Arkansas. For concerns related to our Code of Conduct, please continue with this form.

This form should not be used for emergency matters that require immediate notification. In case of an emergency, please contact us via phone.

To ensure a timely response to your concern, please include all relevant details of your concern such as what happened, when it happened, where it happened, who was involved, who was present, and what each individual did and/or said. If you are reporting multiple concerns please include the detail listed above for each concern.

If you receive any error, delete your browser history and restart your computer.
